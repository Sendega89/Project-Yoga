How to We Do Yogas

Hatha Yoga

In Sanskrit, yoga means "unity", "connection". This is a combination of various spiritual and physical practices to achieve harmony and tranquility. In hatha yoga, the main emphasis is on strengthening the physical capabilities of the body in order to prepare it for spiritual practices.

Fly Yoga

Fly yoga is a trend that combines elements from hatha yoga, pilates, stretching, gymnastics and acrobatics. All exercises are performed using a special hammock.

Stretching

Stretching is a system of exercises aimed at increasing the flexibility of the whole body. By doing stretching, you stretch the muscles of the back, neck, arms, legs, and also work to increase the plasticity and mobility of the joints.
